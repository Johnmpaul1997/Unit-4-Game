You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There will be four crystals below. By clicking on the crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal will be hidden until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will cahnge the value of each crystal.
